Practice Space

Kirikiriroa, New Zealand

 info.

The RVC pratice space, located in Kirikiriroa (Hamilton), Waikato, NZ, is a dedicated rehearsal space for local bands and musicians to practice at a fair price.

With few options in this city, we aim to provide a space where existing and emerging bands can work on new material and prepare for upcoming shows. This fosters the local music scene, and contributes to the overall feeling that things are actually happening in Hamilton. Many great bands come out of our modest city, and that will only continue to happen if they have somewhere to go.

Details:
Basic backline provided.
Cost is $30 per night or $10 per person for bands with 4+ members.
Availability is subject to current bookings, which varies.

For bookings and enquires, email us at realvegancultrecords@gmail.com

RVC is a small collective of four local DIY musicians who occasionally put on shows, release tapes, do screen printing and defend Hamilton as a great place to live.
